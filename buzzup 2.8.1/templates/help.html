BuzzUp is a buzzer round trivia app

A contest where, the player who buzzes first gets to answer first.

BuzzUp has a wide range of topics

With new topics added almost everyday there is literally a lot to buzz about.

You can contribute

You can provide questions on your strong or favourite topic or suggest a new topic.

BuzzUp is social

You can show of the results of a nerve wrecking round to your friends and social group.
